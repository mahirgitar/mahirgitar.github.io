9. . Adam (walk) to school every day + - ? 2. My sister (study) Math once a week + - ? 3. Rafan and his mother (cook) for dinner every night + - ? 4. The dogs (catch) a cat every day + - ? bantu pliss


Jawaban:

1. (+) Adam walks to school every day.

(-) Adam doesn't walk to school every day.

(?) Does Adam walk to school every day?

2. (+) My sister studies Math once a week.

(-) My sister doesn't study Math once a week.

(?) Does my sister study Math once a week?

3. (+) Rafan and his mother cook for dinner every night.

(-) Rafan and his mother don't cook for dinner every night.

(?) Do Rafan and his mother cook for dinner every night?

4. (+) The dogs catch a cat every day.

(-) The dogs don't catch a cat every day.

(?) Do the dogs catch a cat every day?

Penjelasan:

Di Verb ditambahkan s/es ketika Subjectnya She/He/It karena merupakan Simple Present Tense

Simak rumusnya untuk dapat mengubahnya ke dalam bentuk Interrogative, Negative, ataupun Positive.

(+) S + V1 + Obj/Adj (V1 menggunakan s/es tergantung S)

(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + Obj/Adj (V1 tidak diubah)

(?) Do/does + S + V1 + Obj/Adj (V1 tidak diubah)

SIMPLE PRESENT

1.

(+) Adam walks to school everyday.

(- ) Adam doesn't walk to school everyday.

(?) Does Adam walk to school everyday?

2.

(+) My sister studies Math once a week

(-) My sister doesn't study Math once a week.

(?) Does my sister study Math once a week ?

3.

(+) Rafan and his mother cook for dinner every night

(- ) Rafan and his mother don't cook for dinner every night

(?) Do Rafan and his mother cook for dinner every night ?

4.

(+) The dogs catch a cat every day

(- ) The dogs don't catch a cat every day

(?) Do the dogs catch a cat every day

EXPLANATION

Simple present tense digunakan dalam menjabarkan peristiwa yang berulang, aktivitas yang biasa dilakukan sehari-hari, fenomena dan kegiatan yang sudah terjadwalkan dalam waktu dekat.

Penanda kata yang sering digunakan adalah always, sometimes, everyday, once a week, seldom, often, rarely.

always, sometimes, everyday, once a week, seldom, often, rarely.Konstruksi penulisannya :

➡ Verbal (predikatnya berupa kata kerja)

(+) S+verb1(s/es)+O/C

(-) S+do/does+not+Verb1+O/C

(?) Do/does+S+verb1+O/C ?

Contoh

(+) The dogs catch a cat every day

(- ) The dogs don't catch a cat every day

(?) Do the dogs catch a cat every day

Note!

~ Pakai Aux. "do" untuk subyek [I, They, We, You dan kata benda jamak] pd kalimat (-) (?) dan tanpa akhiran -s/es pd kata kerjanya untuk kalimat (+)

~ Pakai Aux "does" untuk subyek [It, She, He, Nama orang] pd kalimat (-) (?) dan menggunakan akhiran -s/es pada kata kerjanya untuk kalimat (+).

~ Khusus kata kerja yang mengandung konsonan -y dan subyeknya tunggal, maka harus dirubah ke bentuk "ie" baru ditambahkan akhiran -s.

35. . Adam (walk) to school every day+-?2. My sister (study) Math once a week+-?3. Rafan and his mother (cook) for dinner every night+-?4. The dogs (catch) a cat every day+-?​


PRESENT SIMPLE

1)

(+) Adam walks to school everyday.

(-) Adam doesn't walk to school everyday.

(?) Does Adam walk to school everyday?

2)

(+) My sister studies math once a week.

(-) My sister doesn't study math once a week.

(?) Does my sister study math once a week?

3)

(+) Rafan and his mother cook for dinner every night.

(-) Rafan and his mother don't cook for dinner every night.

(?) Do Rafan and his mother cook for dinner every night?

4)

(+) The dogs catch a cat every day.

(-) The dogs don't catch a cat every day.

(?) Do the dogs catch a cat every day?

PEMBAHASANSimple Present Tense

Yaitu bentuk tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang sedang berlangsung atau berulangkali.

↬RUMUS

Positive Sentences:

Subject + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + Object

Negative Sentences:

Subject + do/does + not + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + not + Object

Interrogative Sentences:

Do/does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object + ?To be + Subject + Object + ?

↬Auxiliary

DO

Digunakan untuk subjek I/You/We/They dan subjek jamak

DOES

Digunakan untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal (He/She/It)

↬Based on Questions

Complete the sentences into positive, negative, and interrogative!

→ Pada setiap kalimat, terdapat keterangan waktu yang menunjukkan kebiasaan.

Jenis kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris yang menyatakan kebiasaan adalah Present Simple.

→ Untuk mengubah ke bentuk positif, sesuaikan verb dengan subjeknya.

Jika subjeknya tunggal, verb-nya beri akhiran -s/es.Jika subjeknya termasuk I/You/We/They maka verb-nya tidak berakhiran -s/es.

→ Untuk mengubah ke bentuk negatif, sesuaikan auxiliary dengan subjeknya dan beri not setelah aux.

Jika subjeknya tunggal, auxiliary yang digunakan adalah Does.Jika subjeknya termasuk I/You/We/They maka auxiliary yang dipakai adalah Do.

→ Untuk mengubah ke bentuk introgatif, letakkan auxiliary yang sesuai sebelum subjek dan beri tanda tanya di akhir.

Nomor 1 dan 2 subjeknya termasuk tunggal.

(Adam; My sister)

Nomor 3 dan 4 subjeknya termasuk I/You/We/They.

(Rafan and his mother; The dogs)

Contoh:

Nina (listen) to the radio every morning.

→ Subjeknya termasuk tunggal.

Maka verb-nya berakhiran -s/esAuxiliary yang digunakan adalah Does

(+) Nina listens to the radio every morning.

(-) Nina doesn't listen to the radio every morning.

(?) Does Nina listen to the radio every morning?
