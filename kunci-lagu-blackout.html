4. mengubah kalimat acitve ke passive c. visitors may not touch the articles in the museum d. my father was repairing my computer when the blackout onccured e. job seekers are attending a job fair at the town expo center


Kalimat aktif adalah kalimat yang subyeknya melakukan aktivitas atau berbuat sesuatu. Sedangkan kalimat pasif adalah subyeknya tidak melakukan aksi melainkan menerima aksi

Secara umum untuk mengubah kalimat aktif ke kalimat pasif sebagai berikut :

Obyek dalam kalimat aktif menjadi subyek dalam kalimat pasif.Gunakan kata kerja (verb) to be dengan tenses yang sama pada kaimat aktif.Gunakan past participle dari verb pada kalimat aktif.

Berikut ini beberapa rumus untuk mengubah kalimat aktif menjadi kalimat pasif.

Simple Present Tense

Aktif : Subject + Verb 1 + direct object

Pasif : Direct object +is/am/are+ past participle+ by...

Present Continuous Tense

Aktif : S + am/is/are + present participle + direct object

Pasif : S (direct object) + is/am/are + being + past participle +/- by …

Present Perfect Tense

Aktif :  S + have/has + present participle + direct object

Pasif :  S (direct object) + have/has + been + past participle +/- by …

Simple Past Tense

Aktif :    S + verb-2 + direct object

Pasif :   S (direct object) + was/were + past participle +/- by …

Past Continuous Tense

Aktif  :   S + was/were + present participle + direct object

Pasif :  S (direct object) +was/were + being + past participle +/- by …

Simple Future Tense

Aktif :   S + will + verb-1 + direct object

            S + am/is/are going to + bare infinitive + direct object

Pasif :  direct object + will + be + past participle +/- by …

            direct object + am/is/are going to + be + past participle +/- by …


Ubah kalimat dibawah ini dari kalimat aktif menjadi kalimat pasif.

C. Visitor may not touch the articles in the museum. (aktif)

   The Articles in museum may not be touched by the visitor. (pasif)

D. My father was repairing my computer when the blackout occurred. (aktif)

   My computer was being repaired by my father when the blackout occured.     (pasif)

E. Job seekers are attending a job fair at the town expo center. (aktif)

   A job fair at the town expo center is being attended by job seeker. (pasif)
