4. Berapa banyak penyanyi yang membawakan lagu I Believe I Can Fly ?​


1. In 2003, punk rock band Me First and the Gimme Gimmes covered the song for their album Take a Break.

2. American singer William Hung recorded this song for his 2004 album Inspiration.

3. Saxophonist Marion Meadows also covered the song from the album Dressed to Chill.

4. American singer and actress Patti Labelle sang the chorus of the song on her 1998 live album Live One Night Only as an addition to her signature tune "Over the Rainbow".

5. American singer and actress Jessica Simpson sang the song on her DreamChaser Tour in 2001.

6. The song has also been covered by Jane McDonald, Irene Reid, Etta James, Yolanda Adams (in a duet with Gerald Levert), Ruth Brown, James Ingram, Ronan Keating, Bianca Ryan, and formerly of Woe, Is Me Tyler Carter.

7. American Idol candidates Katharine McPhee, Anwar Robinson, Aaron Kelly, Curtis Finch Jr., and Jacob Lusk all performed the song in different seasons of the TV show.

8. Singer Yolanda Adams performed the song at the Ronald McDonald Concert for World Children's Day in 2003.

9. The song was covered by Jermaine Paul on season 2 of The Voice.

10. The song was covered by Tim Olstad on season 3 of The X Factor.

11. The song was covered by Delvin Choice on season 6 of The Voice.

12. The song has been covered live by Faith No More in numerous tours, usually to segue into other songs.

13. The song has been covered live by James Carter's Organ Trio on Out of Nowhere.

Jumlahnya 26 orang.
